What is Sondhan

Stop believing.
Start verifying.

Sondhan is a real-time AI fact-checking app built for the age of misinformation. In seconds, it tells you whether a claim is TRUE, FALSE, or MISLEADING — with evidence, sources, and a confidence percentage.

Social media is full of fake news, misleading headlines, and viral misinformation. Sondhan gives every person the power of a fact-checking team in their pocket.

Powered by OpenAI's GPT-4o and Google's Gemini AI, Sondhan searches the web, cross-checks multiple sources, and gives you a clear, honest verdict — every time.

User Guide

How to use Sondhan.

Step-by-step guide for first-time users.

1
Download and Install

Click the Download APK button. Open the downloaded file on your Android phone and tap Install. Allow unknown sources if prompted.

2
Create Your Account

Open Sondhan and tap Create Account. Enter your name, email, and password. Your account stores fact-check history privately.

3
Choose Input Method

On the home screen, choose the URL, Image, or Text tab depending on how you found the claim you want to check.

4
Paste URL or Upload Image

For URL: copy and paste the link. For Image: select a screenshot from your gallery. For Text: type or paste the claim directly.

5
Tap Fact Check

Hit the blue Fact Check button. Wait 5–10 seconds while Sondhan searches the web. The result appears automatically.

6
Read the Verdict

You'll see TRUE, FALSE, or MISLEADING with a confidence percentage. Scroll to see sources. Tap any source to open it in your browser.

7
Get Summary or Chat with Sondhan AI

Tap Get Summary for a clear 10-sentence breakdown of the full fact-check. Or tap Chat with Sondhan to open the AI chatbot — it already knows everything about your search and answers any follow-up questions about the claim, the sources, or the context.

8
View Your History Anytime

Tap the History tab at the bottom to see all past fact-checks organized by type — URL, Image, and Text checks. Tap any card for full details, or tap Re-check to verify it again with fresh results.
