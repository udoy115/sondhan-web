What is Sondhan

Stop believing.
Start verifying.

Sondhan is a real-time AI fact-checking app built for the age of misinformation. In seconds, it tells you whether a claim is TRUE, FALSE, or MISLEADING — with evidence, sources, and a confidence percentage.

Social media is full of fake news, misleading headlines, and viral misinformation. Sondhan gives every person the power of a fact-checking team in their pocket.

How It Works

From claim to verdict
in seconds.

01
Submit your content
Open Sondhan and choose your input method. Paste a URL, upload a screenshot, or type the claim directly into the text box.
🔗 URL 🖼️ Image 📝 Text
02
AI extracts the claim
For URLs, Sondhan visits the page and reads it. For images, GPT-4o Vision extracts all visible text. For text, it goes directly to verification.
03

User Guide

How to use Sondhan.

Step-by-step guide for first-time users.

1
Download and Install
Click the Download APK button on this page. Open the downloaded file on your Android phone and tap Install. Allow unknown sources if prompted in your phone settings.
2
Create Your Account
Open Sondhan and tap Create Account. Enter your name, email, and password. Your account stores your fact-check history privately.
3
Choose Your Input Method
On the home screen, choose URL, Image, or Text tab depending on how you found the claim you want to check.
4
Paste URL or Upload Image
For URL tab: copy the link and paste it. For Image tab: tap the upload box and select a screenshot from your gallery. For Text tab: type or paste the claim.
5
Tap Fact Check
Hit the blue Fact Check button. Wait 5–10 seconds while Sondhan searches the web. The result will appear automatically below the button.
6
Read the Verdict
You will see TRUE, FALSE, or MISLEADING with a confidence percentage. Scroll down to see the sources. Tap any source to read it in your browser.
7
Get Summary or Chat with Sondhan AI
Tap Get Summary to see a clear 10-sentence breakdown of the full fact-check. Or tap Chat with Sondhan to open the AI chatbot — it already knows everything about your search and will answer any follow-up questions you have about the claim, the sources, or the context.
8
View Your History Anytime
Tap the History tab at the bottom of the app to see all your past fact-checks. They are organized by type — URL checks, Image checks, and Text checks. Tap any card to see the full details, and tap Re-check to verify it again.
